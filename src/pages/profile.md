**Petri Hyde - Advancing Compliance in Information Security Governance**


[Petri Hyde is a risk and compliance professional](https://petrihyde.wordpress.com) with experience in information security, vendor management, and operational resilience. Adept at providing legal services and corporate risk governance, Petri Hyde brings a structured, detail-oriented approach to managing complex compliance environments and safeguarding organizational assets.

In 2022, [Petri Hyde](https://petrihyde.mystrikingly.com/) joined S&A Legal Management Services, Inc. as a risk and controls manager. He serves as the designated policymaker and subject matter expert for several critical domains, including incident security response, vendor management, business continuity planning, data destruction, physical security, and artificial intelligence compliance. His leadership in these areas supports organizational resilience and promotes a proactive approach to risk mitigation. One of his key achievements at the company is overseeing three consecutive years of successful SOC 2 compliance.

Before his current position, he worked as a vendor relations analyst at Scott & Associates, PC. In this role, he managed vendor relationships through a security and compliance lens, data-mapped internal and external information flows, and oversaw remediation of data violations.
