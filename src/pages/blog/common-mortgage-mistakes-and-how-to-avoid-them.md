Securing a mortgage is a very important financial decision. However, despite its importance, borrowers often make mistakes that cost them significant time and money. Mortgage professionals play a crucial role in helping clients navigate the complexities of lending, setting realistic expectations, and avoiding common lending pitfalls. Understanding common errors and adopting proactive strategies to prevent them from happening can result in seamless closings and long-term financial success for homebuyers.\
\
Starting the home shopping process before securing pre-approval is one of the most common mortgage mistakes. Without pre-approval, buyers might become interested in a property that they cannot afford, and they might lose credibility with sellers in competitive markets.
